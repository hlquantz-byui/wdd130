Hello, my name is Hunter Quantz. I am working in the information technology field. I want to transition over to software development after completing my degree. I have tried a bunch of different hobbies and am always looking for new ones. I am also a big fan of privacy and am always looking for ways to increase my privacy online. I was impressed when I read a book called "Data and Goliath" by Bruce Schneier and have been cautious about what I upload to the internet ever since. This is especially true to sites owned by big companies such as Microsoft, Facebook, Google, and Apple. If you haven't read that book it is a great read that can really open your eyes to how much information we are giving away for companies to profit off of. I also think that we end up oversharing information in our daily lives that people end up forgetting unless it stands out in some big way.
