Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this web page is to help potential customers more easily find our company by utilizing SEO, an attractive layout, and a convienent way to contact us.

Audience

The primary target audience of this site is for customers in their early twenties, especially college students looking for an affordable and fun summer getaway. Question 1) Who are the target customers? Describe their life (or business) situation (age, financial situation, interests, and so on) Answer 1: The target customer is a college stuend in their early 20s. They should be looking for an affordable vacation in the outdoors. Question 2) What do they want? Answer 2) They want a fun, memorable experience they can do with their friends that doesn't break the bank. Qustion 3) How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? Answer 3: They will primarily be accessing the site from a mobile device. It is also likely they will use a laptop. The probability they will use a desktop is fairly low.
